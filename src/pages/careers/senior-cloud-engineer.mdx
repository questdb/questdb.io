<h1>Senior Cloud Engineer</h1>

<h2> About QuestDB</h2>

We have built the fastest open source time series database from the ground up to
offer breakthrough performance for real-time analytics using SQL. We bring
experience and technical approaches from low-latency trading to leverage
real-time data processing in various use cases and industries. Our users deploy
QuestDB to make time series analysis fast, efficient, and convenient in
financial services, IoT, application monitoring, and machine learning.

We are a remote-first company based in London and backed by leading venture
capital firms and Y Combinator.

<h2>The role</h2>

As a Senior Cloud Engineer, you will be a critical part of our growing company,
leading the design and launch of a greenfield cloud-hosted database as a service
(DBaaS) solution. Ideally, you have a background as a full-stack software
engineer, and you specialize in cloud-native technologies, distributed systems,
and orchestration platforms. You will bring your expertise to plan, design,
document, and maintain the technologies and infrastructure needed to build a
scalable and secure cloud offering of our time series database. You have an
affinity for best practices in automating operations, infrastructure security,
and software development. You'll have the chance to work in an open and
collaborative environment to improve our existing development tools, technology
stack, and infrastructure along the way.

<h2>Responsibilities</h2>

- Design, implement, and maintain a multi-cloud offering for our customers
- Own the rollout and deployment to customers
- Build monitoring and logging infrastructure
- Build tools to reduce occurrences of errors and improve customer experience
- Design procedures for system troubleshooting and maintenance
- Manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables as part of a
  self-organizing team

<h2>Requirements</h2>

- 5+ years experience working with cloud technologies
- Excellent knowledge of cloud architecture design (e.g., AWS Well-Architected)
- Experience building scalable, resilient, and secured applications
- Experience with CI/CD & Infrastructure as Code (e.g., Terraform)
- Proficient in TypeScript and Node.js. Familiarity with React is a bonus
- Experience with containerized applications and orchestration (e.g., Docker,
  Kubernetes)
- Experience with automated testing
- Good understanding of cloud topology
- Excellent written and communication skills
- Strong team player who is open to guiding colleagues on your areas of
  expertise
- The ability to understand, shape and deliver against customers' requirements

<h2>Working at QuestDB</h2>

We hire talented and passionate people who share our mission to empower
developers to solve their problems with data. We are building breakthrough
technology to power the infrastructure of tomorrow.

- We are a company with thousands of users; our mission is to empower them.
- We invest in a culture that promotes ownership, autonomy and independent
  thinking.
- We have transparent leadership and value employees' strategic inputs.
- Our team is ambitious and tackles the most difficult problems at the deepest
  data infrastructure layer.
